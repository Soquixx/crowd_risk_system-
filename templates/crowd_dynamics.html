Crowd Density

Safe pedestrian density is 1–2 people per m² for comfortable movement.

  • 1–2 p/m²: Comfortable ✓ Safe
  • 3–4 p/m²: Restricted ⚠ Warning
  • 5+ p/m²: Critical ⚡ Danger

Flow Rate
